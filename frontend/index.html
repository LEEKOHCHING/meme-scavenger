⬛ WHAT IS THIS
Meme Scavenge Agent is a Web3 loot-box on BNB Smart Chain. Our AI agent Sophia continuously scans thousands of near-zero meme tokens for signs of life — dev activity, real community signals, on-chain conviction. You pay USDT. You receive a curated token directly in your wallet.
⬛ HOW TO BUY
1. Connect your BSC wallet (MetaMask, OKX, Trust, Binance).
2. Make sure you have USDT (BEP-20) in your wallet.
3. Pick a tier and click BUY.
4. Approve USDT spend → confirm the transaction.
5. Token is swapped and delivered directly to your wallet.

⬛ PLATFORM FEE
A 5% platform fee is charged on every purchase. The remaining 95% is swapped into your token and sent directly to your wallet.

Example: 10 USDT → 0.50 USDT fee → 9.50 USDT swapped to token.
